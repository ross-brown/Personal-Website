About Me

I'm a classicaly trained musicain who has decided to make the jump into the tech industry as a software engineer.

Before discovering my interest in code, I freelanced as a trumpet player in the DFW and Austin area as well as taught private lessons to high school and middle school students. I enjoyed playing a variety of music as well as writing and arranging music on the side as well. As I was finishing my master's degree at Southern Methodist University in Dallas, TX, I began to question whether the life of a musician was what I wanted to do. I stumbled upon a site called "codecademy.com" and gave learning to program a shot, and the rest is history. Being able to experience the thrill of learning something new for the first was exciting and kept me wanting to learn more and more. As I continue to learn and build with code, I hope to one day to work on interesting and exciting projects that significantly impact people's lives and maybe even involve music!

Currently, I am enrolled in a 16-week, full-stack engineering program at Rithm School. I hope to gain valuable experience, meet new people, and build awesome projects as I get closer and closer to my goal of becoming a software engineer.
