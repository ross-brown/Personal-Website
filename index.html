About Me

I'm a full-stack software engineer with a background as a classicaly trained musicain.

Before discovering my interest in code, I freelanced as a trumpet player in the DFW and Austin area, as well as taught private lessons for the past five years. I enjoyed playing a variety of music as well as writing and arranging music on the side. My curiosity to learn new skills led me to discover the world of software engineering. I was hooked right away and developed a passion and interest for coding. Night after night, I found myself teaching myself the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Being able to experience the thrill of learning something new for the first was exciting and kept me wanting to learn more and more. Soon after, I made the decision to pivot my career into software engineering so that I could turn my newfound passion into a career of building, breaking, and learning. As I continue to learn and build with code, I hope to one day to work on interesting and exciting projects that significantly impact people's lives and maybe even involve music!

Currently, I am about to wrap up a 16-week, full-stack engineering program at Rithm School. I've gained valuable experience, met and worked with incredible engineers, and built awesome projects. I am currently looking for job opportunities and hope one day to have an impact on the world and build cool software.
